FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much is entry to The Zoo Park?

Check out our ‘Buy Tickets’ link above for all prices and options for The Zoo entry.

+

How old does my child need to be to receive free of charge entry?

Children are free of charge until the age of One. Children ticket prices apply to all children 1 to 14 years of age.

+

What are your opening hours?

The Zoo is open daily, from July - September we are open from 03:30 PM - 08:00 PM weekends 03:30 - 10:00 PM, October - June weekdays 8:00 AM-8:00 PM weekends 08:00 -10:00 PM . Some of our gorgeous animals take a nap from 12:00 AM - 03:00 PM and some go to bed at 5:30 PM.

+

Is The Zoo open on public holidays?

The Zoo is open 364 days a year.

+

Is the price at the gate the same as tickets purchased online?

The Zoo offers the same price on our website as we do in person at The Zoo Park Entrance.

+

Can I bring my own food and beverage into The Zoo?

Guests are welcome to bring their own food into the Zoo. Please refrain from bringing glass, alcoholic beverages or plastic straws.
